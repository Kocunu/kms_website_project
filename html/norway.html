The modern and luxury Apartment in Oslo

57sqm | 2 Bedrooms | 2 bathrooms | Balcony |


Nestled in the prestigious heart of Oslo, this luxury apartment epitomizes the height of sophisticated living in Norway's esteemed capital. With its modern, chic design, the residence offers a seamless blend of comfort and elegance. As you enter, you are immediately enveloped in warmth by the radiant underfloor heating, a testament to the apartment's no-expense-spared approach to luxury. The living space is bathed in natural light streaming through floor-to-ceiling windows, revealing panoramic views of the Oslo fjord.

The gourmet kitchen, a culinary artist's dream, boasts top-of-the-line appliances and sleek granite countertops. It opens to an airy dining area, perfect for hosting dinner parties that extend into the late hours of the Nordic night.

The master suite is a sanctuary of tranquility, featuring a sumptuous king-sized bed, a walk-in wardrobe, and an opulent en-suite bathroom adorned with marble finishes and designer fixtures. The apartment also includes a smart home system, providing effortless control of lighting, climate, and entertainment with just a touch.
