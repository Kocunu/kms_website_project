The modern and luxury Apartment in Reykjavik

54sqm | 2 Bedrooms | 1 bathroom | Balcony |


Experience the epitome of urban elegance at our luxury apartment, nestled in the heart of Reykjavik, Iceland's vibrant capital. This exquisite property offers a serene retreat from the city's lively pace, without sacrificing the convenience of metropolitan living.
As you step into the apartment, you are greeted by an expansive open-plan living area, bathed in natural light pouring in from floor-to-ceiling windows that offer breathtaking views of the cityscape and the distant mountains.
